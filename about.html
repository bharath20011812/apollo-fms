Story Of Apollo FMS

The story of Apollo Facility Management begins with our founder, Mr. N. Srinivas, who observed a recurring challenge faced by facility managers: the struggle to run their operations smoothly without adequate technical guidance.

He understood that effective facility management requires more than just basic services; it demands skilled manpower and expert oversight. This realization ignited a passion within him to create a solution.
