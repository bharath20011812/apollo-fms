How it Works?

Step 1

Audit and Reporting

  • Conduct a thorough audit of your HVAC system to identify existing leaks and inefficiencies.
  • Generate a detailed report outlining the leakage levels and areas needing attention.
Step 2

Preparation and Setup

  • Cover all duct openings except for one to create a controlled environment.
  • Set up the HVAC duct sealing equipment.
Step 3

Pressurization and Calibration Setup

  • The sealing machine pressurizes the duct interior, creating the necessary conditions for effective sealing.
  • Releases mist containing sealant particles into the duct system.
Step 4

Sealing and Monitoring

  • Sealant particles flow inside the ducts and gradually accumulate near leakages and seal any openings.
  • The machine monitors the sealing process in real time to ensure optimal coverage and effectiveness.
Step 5

Certification

  • Once sealing is complete, a report is generated documenting the process and results.
  • Open the ducts, resume operations, and enjoy improved energy efficiency and air quality.
