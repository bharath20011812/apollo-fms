What the CPCB Policy States?

The Central Pollution Control Board (CPCB) has mandated that diesel generators must be retrofitted with emission control devices to tackle the increasing levels of air pollution. Diesel generators contribute up to 25% of harmful particulate matter, including carbon monoxide and hydrocarbons, which pose serious public health risks.

The policy aims to reduce particulate matter emissions by 70%-90% through appropriate retrofitting technologies
